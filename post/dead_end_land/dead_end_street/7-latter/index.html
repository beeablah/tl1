Dead End Land: Dead End Street (7 - Latter Half)

August 10, 2020 Ensemble Stars , Tomoya Mashiro , Wataru Hibiki
2.3k ~2 min

There is too much going on in this scene. Text alone cannot fully convey how bizarre this is, so I subbed it. I wish I could manipulate the text in-game, but alas, I don't know how.

Series: Ensemble Stars!!
Writer: Seitaro Kino
Characters: Tomoya Mashiro, Wataru Hibiki

Season: Summer
Location: Dead End Café

Wataru
Ladies and gentlemen, please, come take a look! I shall now use my sentient hair to make art with ketchup! ♪
One, two, amazing...☆

Heheh, the dead can control their hair at will! My hair shall be used to serve food as well~☆

Tomoya
(Yeah, all of the customers' reactions were great... The mood's not bad at all.)
Tomoya
(Buuut he can't use theatrics to distract them from the slow service forever.)[1]

(Everyone looks hungry and such... They'd probably prefer character over looks.[2])

(Hmm, it's been a hour since Sengoku and Anzu-san left. I hope they come back soon.)

...Huh, is the manager calling me from the kitchen?

A thumbs up? Uhhh, what's that for—you were on the phone... Sengoku and the others are okay and running over here, meaning they'll be back soon?

Alright. If so, I'll be relaying this to Hibiki-senpai...
(Hibiki-senpai, I think they're coming back soon!)

(Please hold out just a little bit longer!)

Wataru
(Haha. Are you trying to say "Hibiki-senpai, I really like you! You're so cool! I love you!"...?)
(That's too much of a compliment for me. Thank you, Tomoya-kun♪ Kiss...☆)
Tomoya
(Uhm, I think there's a big misunderstanding here?! What's up with that weird signal I got back?)
(Oh, fuck it. This signal can only mean one thing! The rest is up to you!)
Wataru
(Hehe. I got the message.)

(Compared to Mrs. Hidaka's auditions, this is a piece of cake.)

(I am a clown who plays the role expected from him. That's what everyone is looking for, correct?)

Translation Notes

  1. ↑ This line actually has really weird grammar in the original Japanese script that even native speakers (I talked to) couldn't fully understand it. Thanks, Tomoya!
  2. ↑ The idiom used here is "花より団子" (lit. dango rather than flowers), meaning "substance over style." It's localized as "character over looks" to keep the implication of the speaker describing someone he thinks is pretty.

Comments

Wataru really does say "ketchup" despite the sauce shown in his CG being white... It's probably mayo or that weird blend of ketchup and mayo.

No, Tomoya did not literally say "fuck" in the original Japanese script. That is just an editorial decision. Plus, he uses "もう" too much for his own good. With the right tone and context, what a character says can indicate a swear in English.

Tomoya speaks like an "average high school boy." What do average high school boys do? Swear.
