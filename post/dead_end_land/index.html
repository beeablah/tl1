Writer's Comment
I'm the guy who wrote Dead End Land~ While researching to decide on a chapter name, I came across the idiom "生死妄念." Meaning "to be lost[1] in your destructive thoughts forever, even when you reincarnate," which suits Mayoi perfectly! He's literally the personification of it! (He's not)
Seitaro Kino [@kinosei]. August 11, 2020.
Translation Notes
  1. ↑ "迷い" (mayoi) is translated as "lost" here, but it can also mean doubt, delusion, etc. In Buddhism, it refers to the inability to reach enlightenment.
