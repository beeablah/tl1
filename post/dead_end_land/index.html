Writer's Comment

I was the guy in charge of writing Dead End Land~ While researching to decide on a chapter name, I came across the idiom "生死妄念." Meaning "to be lost[1] in your destructive thoughts forever, even when you reincarnate," which suits Mayoi perfectly! He's literally the personification of it! (He's not) ➹

Translator's Notes

  1. ↑ "迷まよい" (/ma̠jo̞i/ mayoi) is translated as "lost" here, but it can also mean doubt, delusion, etc. In Buddhism, it refers to the inability to reach enlightenment.
