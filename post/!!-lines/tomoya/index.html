Profile

A true actor makes the impossible possible and turns dreams into reality.

Normalcy and Seriousness Made Charming
An average guy, for better or worse. Besides being an idol, he is also an aspiring actor. While Tomoya is serious and caring, he is easily swayed by his surroundings. With a mild, humble voice, he delivers energetic, vigorous performances. The leader of Rhythm Link's Ra*bits.

  • Class: Yumenosaki Academy, 2-B
  • Circles: Dramatica, ANIMALS
  • Height 168 cm
  • Weight: 53 kg
  • Blood Type: A
  • Age: 16 (on July 1)–17
  • Birthday: March 29 (Aries)
  • Hobbies: Listening to music, reading manga
  • Specialty: Liked by animals
  • Image Color: ★ (#EEDCB3)
Tomoya
I'm Tomoya Mashiro from Rhythm Link and the unit Ra*bits, which has a theme of being "small and cute." I might be more "average" than everyone else... However, my willingness to put a smile on all our fans' faces is unmatched! I'm still a bit unreliable, but I'll definitely grow into a magnificent idol that you'll be proud of, so please give me your support! Thank you...♪
Mitsuru
Tomo-chan's kinda like a mom, since he's always naggin' me around. But he only tells me off when I do somethin' bad, so I'm super thankful for that~♪ It always feels like I'm gonna dash off and leave everyone behind, so Tomo-chan being there to hold me back is a really good thing! I wanna be friends with him forever and ever~!
